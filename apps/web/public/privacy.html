Who we are

haloFrame is operated by {{COMPANY_LEGAL_NAME}}. We make AI-assisted memorial tributes from photos you choose. This policy explains what we collect, why, and what you can ask us to do with it. Questions? Write to {{CONTACT_EMAIL}}.

What we collect

Account information (email + display name), the photos you upload, the tributes you save, usage metadata (device type, timestamps, error traces), and payment metadata from our processor (we never store your card number). If you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive the identifier they send us — nothing more.

How we use it

To deliver the product you asked for: process your photo through our AI pipeline, save the finished tribute to your gallery, fulfill canvas orders, and support you if something goes wrong. We never train models on your photos, and we never sell your personal information.

AI processing partner

Photos you upload are sent to fal.ai (https://fal.ai), our AI processing partner, to generate composite portraits. Photos are encrypted in transit, processed once, and not used to train AI models. fal.ai’s privacy policy: https://fal.ai/privacy.

Where it lives

Photos and tributes live in our Supabase storage. Payment records live with our payment processor. Usage logs live in our operational analytics. Retention: tributes are kept until you delete them or delete your account. Operational logs are kept for 90 days. When you delete your account, all photos and tributes are removed within 30 days.

Your rights (GDPR + CCPA)

You can download a copy of your data anytime (Settings → Export my data), edit your display name, delete individual tributes, or delete your entire account (Settings → Delete account). Under GDPR Articles 15 and 17, EU residents have the right of access and erasure. Under CCPA §1798.100/.105, California residents have the same rights. Write to {{CONTACT_EMAIL}} for help.

Third parties we rely on

Supabase (auth, database, storage), fal.ai (AI model inference), Stripe (web payments), RevenueCat (mobile subscriptions), Resend (transactional email), Vercel (web hosting), Railway (API hosting), Sentry (error monitoring). Each is contractually bound to handle your data only for the purpose we asked them to.

Changes to this policy

If we change anything material, we’ll notify you by email and update the "Last updated" date above. Continued use after the effective date means you accept the update.
